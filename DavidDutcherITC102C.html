A Brief History Of Me







Greetings! I'm David. I was born in New Mexico, but I've also lived in California, Florida, Alaska, and Vietnam. I have lived in Seattle for 18 years now. I love music, movies, parkour and MMA. I have a Bachelor's Degree of Social Work from NMSU and now I'm studying Web Design at Seattle Central College. I also work for the YWCA in the housing division. For this project, I am using CSS. Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) are what create the look and feel of a web page. Cascading was developed in a priority-schemed fashion to determine how a rule is applied to more than one element. Additionally, it enables accessibilty, such as speech-based browsing and it can be used on Braille-based devices.
